🔗 Connect Google Sheets Database

1
Open Google Sheets → create a spreadsheet named GreekToGo POS Database with tabs: Inventory, SalesLog, ShiftLog
2
In your Sheet go to Extensions → Apps Script, paste the GreekToGo_AppScript.js code, and click Save.
3
Click Deploy → New Deployment → Web App. Set Execute as: Me and Who has access: Anyone. Copy the URL.
4
Paste the deployment URL below and click Connect.
